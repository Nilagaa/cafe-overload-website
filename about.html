About Us

Cafe Overload is a cozy, modern cafe located in the heart of the city. We specialize in serving high-quality coffee, delicious pastries, and a range of drinks that cater to all tastes. Our mission is to provide a relaxing space where our customers can unwind, work, and enjoy a good cup of coffee.
